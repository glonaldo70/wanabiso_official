## A Professional Marketplace

Kwata is evolving into a premier e-commerce platform designed to bridge the gap between local sellers and buyers. With our latest update, we have professionalized the entire experience to ensure trust and efficiency.

### For Buyers: "Mode Acheteur"

Finding what you need has never been easier.

- **Precise Search**: Define your needs with specific criteria using our new "Spécifications" feature.
- **Clear Pricing**: See "Tarification" details upfront, including delivery costs.
- **Delivery Options**: Choose between home delivery or pickup points.

### For Sellers: "Vendeur Kwata"

Empower your business with professional tools.

- **Showcase Your Products**: Use "Photos du Produit" to present high-quality images.
- **Manage Your Business**: Track your earnings and transactions in your "Portefeuille de Crédits".
- **Reach More Customers**: Benefit from a platform optimized for visibility and conversion.

### Trust and Security

We prioritize your safety. Our updated "Conditions Générales d'Utilisation" and strict verification processes ensure a secure environment for all transactions. Whether you are buying or selling, Kwata provides the framework for successful commerce.

Join the revolution today and experience the new standard of local e-commerce with Kwata.
